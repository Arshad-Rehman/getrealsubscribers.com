Create YouTube Channel Art: The Important Guidelines To Follow

Before creating the banner, you have to follow the dimension of the channel art.

  • The size of YT channel art should be with the dimension of 2048 X 1152 and the aspect ratio should be 16:9.
  • You should place the text and logos within the specified area 1235 X 338 pixels. A bigger picture does not fully appear on devices.
  • The file size of the banner should be less than 6MB.

The dimension of banner should be different in gadgets:

  • The dimension of banner in Mobile should be1546px * 423px and in Tablet 1855px * 423px and same as Desktop 2560px * 423px, in TV 2560px * 1440px.

Small fonts are not clear to understand. We highly recommend you use large, clear, and bold fonts to capture the attention of the visitor on your channel. Doing it helps the viewers to see the important points and you should also use a call to action word in the banner.
