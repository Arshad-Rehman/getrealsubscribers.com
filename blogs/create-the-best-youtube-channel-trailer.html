How To Create A YouTube Channel Trailer In 2022?

Creating a trailer for your YouTube channel is quite easy. Just follow the steps given below and you will be good to go.

  • First, sign in to YouTube Studio.
  • Go to the menu option situated on the left and click Customization Layout.
  • Below the Video option, click ADD.
  • Now pick a video for your channel trailer.
  • Click Publish.

Requirements For A Successful YouTube Channel Trailer Video

There are certain points to keep in mind while creating a trailer video. This is very important so do not skip any of the steps mentioned below.

  • Introduce yourself and your channel. An introduction should be short and to the point.
  • Talk about your motive and how your channel will help the viewers to gain knowledge, fun, or any other sort of entertainment.
  • Choose a video schedule and talk about it. Your viewers should know about the timing of your future uploads.
  • Add a call to action at least 2 times in your video. ‘Please like and subscribe to my channel’ is the most common CTA used by YouTubers.
