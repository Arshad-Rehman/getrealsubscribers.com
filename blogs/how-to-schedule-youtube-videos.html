How To Schedule YouTube Videos On Your Desktop?

  1. Go to the top right corner of YouTube. Click on the camera icon. Or if you’re in YouTube studio, then you can click on the red Create button.
  2. Drag and drop your video to upload.
  3. It’s time to add a title, a description, and a thumbnail image. Select a playlist too for your video if you wish.
  4. Now you will get the options to either save, publish or schedule your video. Before choosing a schedule, you need to first choose the listing preference. Select private or public. If you select public, your uploaded content will go live.
  5. Click schedule. Enter the date and time you would like your video to go live.

That’s it. You will get a notification that your video has been scheduled successfully.

How To Schedule YouTube Videos On Your Phone?

  1. Download two apps for this purpose: the YouTube app and the YouTube Studio app.
  2. Open the YouTube app and click on the camera icon.
  3. Choose the video to be uploaded.
  4. Click next.
  5. Add title, description, and location.
  6. Mark the video as private.
  7. Click upload.
  8. Switch to YouTube studio app.
  9. You will get to see the video which you just uploaded.
  10. Click on the pencil icon in the top right-hand corner. This is the edit button.
  11. Now click on schedule.
  12. Enter the date and time you would like your video to go public.
  13. Click Save.

That’s it. Your video will be scheduled successfully.

How To Edit Scheduled Videos

  • Navigate to your uploaded videos on YouTube and click on the edit tool.
  • Edit your video’s title, description, and other details as required.
  • Click on Editor to make changes to the video.

Writing a detailed description is very important because it lets viewers know the main concept of your video. Do not go overboard with it. Make it to the point so that the content doesn’t take a lot of time to read.

Look how your competitors publish their videos and interact with their viewers. Look at how they promote their content. This will help you to level up your game and beat them with better strategies.
