What Is YouTube Watch Time?

In simple words, watch time is the total amount of time that viewers spend watching your videos. Many people assume that views and watch time are the same thing but on the contrary, it’s not.

Views are just the clicks on your videos but they don’t tell you for how long the visitors have watched your content. On the other hand, watch time gives you detailed information for exactly how long the viewers have spent watching your videos.

So, why is watch time important for YouTube?

YouTube makes money through the ads it shows to users. The more watch time it has, the more ads it can display to the audience. This helps the platform to earn money as well as help the video creator to gain monetary benefits.

The more YouTube watch hours you can create from your videos, the more you will be rewarded by the most popular video-sharing app. Additionally, the platform will also promote your channel through search and recommended videos. Hence a win-win situation for both YouTube and the creator.
